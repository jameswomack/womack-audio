Womack Audio

This installer will install the Womack Audio plugins as Audio Units (AU) and/or VST3 plugins. Use the Customize button to choose which to install.

  • Womack FX — fuzz, univibe, and tape delay. In Logic Pro: Audio FX → Womack → Womack FX.
  • Womack Resonote — note-aware resonant filter with scale snapping. In Logic Pro: Audio FX → Womack → Womack Resonote (accepts MIDI to track pitch).

After installation, restart your DAW.

System requirements: macOS 12 or later, 64-bit DAW.
